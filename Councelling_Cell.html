Counselling Cell

About the Counsellor :


  • Name : Beatrice Lobo
    • (Master in Psychology) The counsellor has been working with the college since 2003.

Objective :


  • To promote a positive attitude among students.
  • To help students cope with personal and other issues.
  • To help enhance student success by promoting student-teacher- parent relations.

How can the counselor help you?


  • Provide the right guidance.
  • Career counseling.
  • Support with academic and personal issues.
  • Assist with enhancing program concentration and study skills to help a student balance his/her activities.

Making an appointment:


  • Any student who would like to visit the counsellor can walk in anytime between 8 a.m. to 4 p.m.
  • Students can also see the counsellor based on staff recommendation
  • Any parent who would like to meet the counsellor would have to make a prior appointment for the same.
